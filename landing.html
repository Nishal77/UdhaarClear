Will sending automated reminders damage my client relationship? +
Not if it's done right — and that's exactly what our AI Tone Engine is designed for. Phase 1 is so warm and professional that customers often thank businesses for the reminder. Escalation only happens if the customer ignores the previous message entirely. You can also mark VIP customers for always-gentle treatment, and the engine pauses the moment a customer responds or taps the payment link.
Is the WhatsApp API official? Will my account get banned? +
UdhaarClear uses the official Meta-certified WhatsApp Business API — not grey routes or unofficial tools. All messages use pre-approved templates. Your business name is verified. 100% compliant with Meta's messaging policies. Zero risk of ban.
How does the MSME legal notice actually work? +
When an invoice crosses Day 22 unpaid, UdhaarClear auto-generates a formal legal notice citing Section 16 of the MSME Development Act 2006, with compound interest at 3× RBI bank rate calculated to the day. It's sent via WhatsApp and stored as a PDF. 89% of businesses pay within 72 hours of receiving this notice — without going to court. No lawyer needed.
We use Tally. Can UdhaarClear work with it? +
Right now, you can export your outstanding report from Tally as an Excel or CSV file and import up to 500 invoices at once into UdhaarClear — it takes under 5 minutes. Direct Tally ERP sync (zero-export, real-time) is coming in Q3 2026 for Growth and Scale plan users.
Is my customer data safe? Where is it stored? +
All data is encrypted at rest (256-bit AES) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Hosted on AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) — your data never leaves India. We never store customer payment credentials — all payments are handled by Razorpay, which is RBI-regulated and PCI-DSS compliant.
What happens after the free trial ends? +
Nothing abrupt. You'll get a reminder 3 days before your trial ends. If you choose not to subscribe, reminders pause — your data is never deleted. You can subscribe and resume at any time. No hidden fees, no auto-charge without explicit consent.
